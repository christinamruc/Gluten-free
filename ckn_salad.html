Chicken Salad

This is a delicious chicken salad with a hint of tart from the Granny Smith apples and sweet from the cranberries. Delicious served on toasted gluten free bread or with gluten free pretzles.

INGREDIENTS
  1. 2 chicken breasts, baked and cooled, chopped into bite size pieces
  2. 1/2 c mayonnaise
  3. 1/2 c dried cranberries
  4. 2 stalks celery, finely chopped
  5. 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  6. 1/2 green apple, grated
  7. 1/2 c walnuts or almonds, finely chopped
  8. salt and pepper to taste
DIRECTIONS
  1. Mix all ingredients together in large bowl. Chill in refridgerator before serving. Serve on gluten free bread or with gluten free pretzles.
