Zucchini Soup

Creamy soups have become a new favorite and this one is smooth and creamy without any dairy. Adjust seasonings to taste. I find it easier to puree in small batches and then mix it together at the end.

INGREDIENTS
  1. 2 to 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  2. 1 large onion, chopped
  3. 2 to 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  4. 8 to 10 cups chopped zucchini (about 5 to 6 medium zukes)
  5. 6 tablespoons uncooked white rice
  6. 8 cups chicken stock or vegetable stock
  7. 1 bunch cilantro, coarsely chopped
  8. 2 to 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  9. sea salt and pepper to taste
DIRECTIONS
  1. Heat a 6 to 8-quart pot over medium heat. Add the olive oil, then the onion and sauté for 5 to 10 minutes or until onion softens and is beginning to change color. Add garlic and sauté a minute more. Then add chopped zucchini, white rice, and chicken stock. Cover and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes or until zucchini is tender and rice is cooked.
  2. Add cilantro and lemon juice to the pot. Remove from heat and puree in your blender in batches. Pour soup into another clean pot or bowl, stir batches together, and taste. If it needs a flavor boost, add sea salt or pepper to taste.
